Project Functionality

Main features of the HealthSync project

User-friendly Interface

Provides a simple and easy-to-use website for patients and users.

Health Information Management

Allows users to view and manage health-related details digitally.

Doctor/Patient Support

Helps users connect health services and basic medical information.

AI Assistance

Uses Google AI features to provide smart responses or guidance related to health queries.

Appointment/Service Access

Helps users access healthcare services more easily through the website.

Responsive Website

Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.

Fast and Easy Access

Reduces manual work and saves time by keeping health services online.

Secure Digital Platform

Keeps user information organized and improves communication between users and healthcare services.
